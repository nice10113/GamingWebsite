Dragonball FighterZ

Dragon Ball FighterZ[a] (pronounced "fighters"[1]) is a 2.5D[2] fighting game developed by Arc System Works and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Based on the Dragon Ball franchise, it was released for the PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Microsoft Windows in most regions in January 2018, and in Japan the following month, and was released worldwide for the Nintendo Switch in September 2018.
